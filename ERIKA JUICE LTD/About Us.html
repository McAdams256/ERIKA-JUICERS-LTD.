ABOUT US

BUSINESS OVERVIEW

The World juice market is expected to grow pass 200 million metric tons by 2025, driven by the surging popularity of healthy, organic and unprocessed fresh fruit juice. Fresh fruit and low-calorie juices are especially poised to benefit against the backdrop of growing consumer focus on health and fitness.

Rising markets offer opportunities in the form of greater potential for higher market penetration and growth in per capita consumption, while in the developed markets, innovation in new flavours and healthy ingredients will spruce up consumption.


JUICE RODUCTION

Erika juicers' main goal is to provide original fresh fruit juice for our consumers in a way that is more sustainable and more cost effective than what our competitors are offering in the wide industry.

We at Erika Juicers Ltd. will be able to supply our consumers with fresh fruit juice which is very affordable compared to that of our competitors. Our focus at Erika juicers ltd. will predominantly be on business to business selling whereby we will work as a manufacturer.

Erika Juicers Ltd. will do business from one major site in Uganda. We believe that this facility will serve as a centre for distributing our fresh fruit juice across borders where need be of shipping around East Africa.
