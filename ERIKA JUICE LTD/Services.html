OUR SERVICES

  1. WHOLE SALE
    • Here a customer is given chance to sell our products as a wholesaler and we as a company have an agreement with this customer on terms and conditions of their patnering with us as wholesalers. This will in account add a wholesale bonus given to them.

  2. RETAIL SALE
    • Here we as a company become distributors to customers who would like to sell our products as retailers. This package same as the wholesale package, comes with a retailer's bonus.

  3. SUBSCRIPTION
    • Here the customer comes to an agreement with the management on the product linked to the subscription service; this being the glass bottled juice and our fixed area suppliers will ensure that all these services are availed to customers according to the period agreed upon for delivery

UPDATES; The subscription is still under review by the board currently for customers outside Uganda but communication will be sent out through our social media platforms.
